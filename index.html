How We Work

At Standfix Kitchen, we make meal ordering and catering stress-free, seamless, and reliable — whether it’s indoor meals, outdoor events, or full-service catering.

Step 01

Share Your Food Request

Tell us your meal needs — event size, preferred menu, budget, or daily food plan. We help you choose the best options tailored to your taste and occasion.

Step 02

Menu Planning & Confirmation

We work with you to finalize the menu — soups, stews, local dishes, continental meals, grilled foods, small chops, or full buffet catering. Everything is planned to perfection.

Step 03

Fresh & Professional Meal Preparation

Your meals are cooked fresh, same-day, in a clean environment using quality ingredients. Every dish is prepared with care to deliver great taste and consistency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions people ask about meal orders, event catering, pricing, and service support at Standfix Kitchen.

1 Do you offer both indoor and outdoor catering?

Yes, Standfix Kitchen caters for indoor dining, home meal delivery, small gatherings, and full outdoor events such as weddings, birthdays, corporate functions, church programs, and more.

2 How early should I book for an event?

For small events, we recommend booking at least 3–5 days ahead. For weddings, corporate events, and large outdoor catering, booking 2–4 weeks ahead ensures the best preparation and service.

3 Do you deliver daily meals?

Yes, we deliver freshly cooked home-style meals daily to homes, offices, students, and organisations. We also offer weekly subscription meal plans.

1 What determines your catering prices?

Pricing depends on the menu selection, guest size, event location, serving style, and additional services such as ushers, buffet setup, decoration, and rentals (if required).

2 Do you offer budget-friendly catering packages?

Yes, Standfix Kitchen provides flexible and affordable catering packages for different budgets — from simple meals to premium full-event setups.

3 Can I order in bulk for parties or offices?

Absolutely! We handle bulk food orders for offices, schools, seminars, parties, and all types of gatherings — including rice dishes, proteins, soups, grills, pastries, and small chops.

1 Do you handle serving and on-site support during events?

Yes, our team can provide full on-site support including professional servers, buffet arrangement, food warming setups, table service, and crowd management during events.

2 How can I reach Standfix Kitchen for quick inquiries?

You can reach us through our contact form, WhatsApp, direct phone call, or any of our social pages. Our support team responds quickly to meal orders and event inquiries.

Still have questions? We’re here to help!
